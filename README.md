## Overview

**Startup Survival** is an interactive browser-based decision game that simulates real-world startup leadership challenges.

The player takes on the role of a startup leader and must navigate a series of high-pressure scenarios involving:
- Deadlines
- Team conflict
- Product decisions
- Hiring
- Strategic pivots

Each decision directly impacts key startup metrics, forcing the player to balance trade-offs under time pressure.

---

## Objective

Survive all rounds while maintaining a healthy balance between:

- **Speed**
- **Quality**
- **Morale**
- **Burnout**

There are no perfect decisions — only trade-offs. The goal is to finish with the strongest possible overall outcome.

---

## Gameplay

- The game consists of **6 rounds**, each representing a realistic startup scenario
- Each round:
  - Presents a challenge
  - Offers **3 decision options**
  - Includes a **20-second timer**
- If time runs out → a random decision is made
- Immediate feedback is provided after each choice
- A **decision log** tracks the player’s journey

---

## Learning Goals

This prototype is designed to reinforce key entrepreneurial mindset concepts:

- Decision-making under uncertainty  
- Trade-offs between speed, quality, and team wellbeing  
- Leadership and communication  
- Managing burnout and morale  
- Strategic thinking (pivot vs iterate)
